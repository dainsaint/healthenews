Heal The News

The Philadelphia Inquirer

Sunlight is the best disinfectant

On June 8th, 2020, Journalists of Color at the Inquirer released a letter detailing five immediate actions for the company to take towards a more equitable newsroom. We demanded transparency in the actions the Inquirer would take to address those actions. We have created this site as a way to practice that transparency in our newsroom. As our industry undergoes a seismic shift spurred by the voices of underrepresented reporters that have gone unheard for decades, we hope this action inspiries similar calls for transparency in media companies across the country.
